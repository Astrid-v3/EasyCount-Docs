# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: July 16, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how EasyCount handles information when used on Discord.

## Information We Collect

When you use the bot, it may store the following information:

* Discord user IDs
* Discord server IDs
* Discord channel IDs
* Statistics related to bot features (for example, counting progress, correct answers, or mistakes)

The bot does not collect:

* Passwords
* Discord messages outside of what is required for bot functionality
* Personal information such as your real name, address, or email address

## How We Use Information

The stored information is used only to provide and improve bot features, such as:

* Keeping track of server-specific settings
* Saving user statistics
* Maintaining progress and game data

## Data Storage

Data is stored securely by the bot owner. The bot only stores the minimum amount of information required for its features.

## Data Removal

If you want your server's data or your user statistics removed, contact the bot owner with your request. The relevant data will be deleted where possible.

## Third-Party Services

The bot uses Discord's services to communicate with users and servers. Discord's own Privacy Policy applies to information handled by Discord.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the bot changes or new features are added. Continued use of the bot after changes means you accept the updated policy.

## Contact

For questions or data removal requests, contact astrid.v3 on discord.
